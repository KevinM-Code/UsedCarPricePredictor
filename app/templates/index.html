This is an application to help executives at RentMobility to make more informed decisions about the the cars they would like to purchase or obtain the current asking prices for particular used cars they may want to sell from the fleet.

Dataset Analysis



Data Validity and Relationship

In this box plot you can see the correlation between the age of the car and the milage. This is the result of a 90,286 used car dataset that was collect by web scraping Craigslist car listings. This is an indication that our dataset is valid and will give us some good predictions.

Suspect data may not have mileage increase as the age of the car increases.

Price Distribution

This graph shows that 61.2% of the cars in the dataset are listed between $5,000 and $20,000.

Car Model Popularity

The graph gives inventory managers an indication as to the popularity of the manufacturer of the cars in the dataset. This information could be useful in deciding how many of each make of car to have available.

Predicted Price vs. Actual Price

As you can see that the price will be very close to the actual, giving use high confidence that the program will be able to give you an excellent estimation.
